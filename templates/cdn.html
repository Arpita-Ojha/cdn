Hey Guys ! This is BTS

Bangtan Sonyeondan

BTS is also known as the Bangtan Boys. It is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed in 2013 in Seoul. Bangtan Sonyeondan in Korean means "bulletproof boy scouts." Koreans call them by this name, however people in other countries refer to them as BTS.

Army welcomed purple as BTS' color, and their way of telling BTS how much they love them.
